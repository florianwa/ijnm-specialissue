Scope of the Special Issue

Description:

In recent years, the heterogeneity and variability of Internet applications - whether in the multimedial or interactive environment - has increased significantly. Nowadays, video streaming is responsible for a large portion of all bandwidth on the Internet, and Internet telephony and video conferencing systems have become part of our daily life. At the same time, the Internet of Things (IoT) strives for the first place in terms of the number of devices, and smartphones and tablets contribute to the ever-growing demands on the networks. Among other things, the spread of web browsing and web-based services allows for increasing acceptance and development of video games, upcoming virtual reality applications, 360° video and much more.
